Privacy Policy for FinAIx


Effective Date: September 22, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how FinAIx (the "App") collects, uses, and handles your information when you use our mobile application.



Information We Collect


The App is designed to function with data stored exclusively on your device. We do not have servers, and we do not collect or store your personal information remotely. The App handles the following types of information:

  • Personally Provided Financial Information: To provide personalized analysis and financial health assessments, the App allows you to voluntarily enter information about your financial profile. This includes your age, life stage, annual income, risk tolerance, investment goals, assets (like account types, holdings, and balances), and liabilities. This information is saved only on your device.
  • Third-Party Credentials: The App requires you to enter your own personal API keys for various third-party financial data services. For the SEC EDGAR integration, you are required to provide a contact email address, which is used in the API request header as required by the SEC. These keys and your email are stored securely in the App's private storage on your device.



How We Use Your Information


The information handled by the App is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To Operate Core App Functionality: Your API keys are used to make direct requests from your device to the respective third-party services (e.g., Finnhub, Groq) to fetch market data, news, and financial analysis. Without these keys, the App cannot function.
  • To Personalize Your Experience: The financial profile information you provide is used locally by the App's AI features to generate tailored insights, financial analysis, stock recommendations, and portfolio suggestions. This data is processed on-device or sent directly to your chosen AI provider (e.g., Groq, Gemini) as part of a prompt, but it is not collected or viewed by the developers of FinAIx.



Data Storage and Security


All information you provide to FinAIx is stored exclusively on your device within the App's private storage area using Android's standard SharedPreferences system.

  • We do not have access to your financial profile, watchlist, or API keys.
  • Since your data is not uploaded to our servers, we cannot share it with any other parties.
  • You are responsible for the security of your own device.



Third-Party Services


The App acts as a client that connects to various third-party data providers using the API keys you provide. Your interactions with these services are subject to their respective privacy policies and terms of service. We encourage you to review them. These services include, but are not limited to:

  • Groq
  • Google Gemini
  • Finnhub
  • Polygon.io
  • SEC EDGAR
  • FRED (Federal Reserve Economic Data)
  • Alpha Vantage
  • EODHD (E-O-D Historical Data)
  • FMP (Financial Modeling Prep)
  • Tiingo
  • Twelve Data
  • PredictHQ
  • Trading Economics



User Control and Data Deletion


You have complete control over your data. You can edit or delete any information within your financial profile at any time directly within the App. Uninstalling the App will permanently delete all its data from your device.



Children's Privacy


The App is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.



Changes to This Privacy Policy


We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the App or its store listing. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
